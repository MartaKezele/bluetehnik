About us

We are the Blue tehnik d.o.o., a company engaged in the production of packaging made of soft PU, PE, and EPE foam. Let us briefly introduce ourselves to you:

Blue tehnik d.o.o. was founded in 2018 when Boris Prevol≈°ek, owner and director, took over the production of soft polyurethane (PU), polyethylene foam (PE, EPE) and closed cell ethylene-vinyl acetate copolymer foam (EVA foam) which is used for packaging and toys.

We started as a small family business and due to flexibility and quick responses, we easily adapt to all market conditions. In this way, our customers are provided with the highest standards of product and service quality. We cherish an individual approach to our clients which allows us to successfully find solutions to all problems our clients might face. Experience and expertise allow us to fulfill our mission and meet all the wishes and requirements our customers have.

At Blue tehnik, we understand the needs of our clients are different, so together with them, we design the best possible solutions and thus ensure that they are always in line with their needs and wants. Our company is proud to have a stable financial position and quality service.

In Slovenia, we are present in all Slovenian regions.

High-quality products and services we strive to provide is not just a phrase but it represents our mission and way of working. Criteria such as quality, flexibility, and customer focus are not self-evident and require constant efforts of all employees. It is precisely because of these qualities that our customers like to come back to us, which we are proud of and we want to do the same in the future.

Our plant allows us different ways of processing different foams:

  • Saw Cutting (horizontal and vertical)
  • Cutting according to model (suitable for packaging)
  • Die Cutting
  • Gluing

If you need packaging made of soft PU foam or PE and EPE foam to protect your products during transport, or you want additional information and advice on options and choosing the most suitable packaging for your products, we will be happy to respond to your invitation to present our products and give you an inquiry.
